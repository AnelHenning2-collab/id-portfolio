Understanding CAC & LTV

Quick Question

A local coffee shop spends $200 on Instagram ads and gets 10 new customers.

Did that work out?

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

That's Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) — what you spend, on average, to gain one new customer.

CAC Needs a Partner: LTV

CAC alone doesn't tell you if that number is a good deal. You also need Lifetime Value (LTV) — what a customer is worth over time. A healthy business generally wants LTV to be 3x or more than CAC.
